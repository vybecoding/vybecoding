Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with vybecoding?

Getting started is easy! First, create a free account by clicking the "Sign Up" button. Once you're logged in, browse our guides and choose a topic that interests you. We recommend starting with our "Introduction to AI Development" guide if you're new to AI programming.

What's included in the Pro subscription?

Pro subscribers get access to:

  • All premium guides and tutorials
  • 1-on-1 mentorship sessions
  • Priority support
  • Early access to new features
  • Exclusive community channels
  • Downloadable resources and code templates
How do mentorship sessions work?

Mentorship sessions are 1-on-1 video calls with experienced developers. You can browse available mentors, check their expertise and availability, then book a session that fits your schedule. Sessions typically last 30-60 minutes and can cover code reviews, career advice, or specific technical topics.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked, and you can resubscribe anytime.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Yes! We offer custom plans for teams and enterprises with features like centralized billing, team management, custom learning paths, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team at enterprise@vybecoding.ai for more information.

Still need help?

Our support team is here to help. Reach out to us and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
